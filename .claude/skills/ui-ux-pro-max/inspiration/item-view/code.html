Provenance & History

"This specific note was part of the prestigious Aldrich Collection, held in private hands for over forty years before appearing at the 2012 New York International Numismatic Convention."

The 1928 Gold Certificates represent the last small-size gold currency issued by the United States before the 1933 gold recall. Their distinctive yellow seals and serial numbers serve as a reminder of an era when currency was directly redeemable in gold coin at the United States Treasury.
